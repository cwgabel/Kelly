Using Google Lens in Chrome

Kel, you can click on any area within the address bar in Chrome to activate the Google Lens icon (located on the far right side).

Then, click the icon once more to enter this mode, where you can select the text you want to convert into actual text with your mouse.

Once you click on the 'copy text' prompt, the selected text will be placed in your clipboard. Then you can paste it whereever you please.

When you are done with Google Lens, either press the Escape key or move the mouse to the top area of the screen and click your mouse.

Have a nice day!
